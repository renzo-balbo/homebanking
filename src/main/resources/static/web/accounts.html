How can I apply for a loan?

Applying for a personal loan is similar to applying for a credit card. You'll need to enter your personal information, your financial information and the details about your desired loan. Before approving you, the lender will run a hard credit check, which may temporarily lower your credit score. If your financial picture and credit score are sufficient for the lender — often, you need a credit score in the mid-600s — the lender will set your interest rate, loan amount and terms
